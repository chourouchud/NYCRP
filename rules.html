GAME RULES

  1. Random Death Match (RDM): You are not allowed to start shooting randomly at other players‎ ‎for no apparent reason or roleplay beforehand.
  2. Vehicle Death Match (VDM): Do not hit/ram, anyone, with your car for no reason.
  3. Fail Roleplay (FRP): Failing to Roleplay or breaking a Roleplay by randomly taking out your gun and shooting, ETC is not allowed. No Roleplay Realism Also falls under FRP.
  4. New Life Rule (NLR): When you die in the game, you should forget all the previous‎ memories of your past life and start a new one.
  5. Leaving to avoid Punishment (LTAP): You are not allowed to leave the game when you are currently being punished by a staff member. Doing so could result in a ban.
  6. Full Kill (FKL): You are not allowed to fully kill someone if there are no EMS in-game. You must stop at half health unless there are EMS online.
  7. Cuff Rushing/Auto-arresting: You must say cuffs when arresting someone. You then need to transport the suspect to the county jail, you cannot auto-arrest them.
  8. Tow Rushing: Rushing to tow a car with no Roleplay or DOT called isn't allowed.
  9. Cop Baiting: You must not purposefully commit a crime to grab an officer's attention.
  10. Safe Zone: Crimes are prohibited in the following areas: Police Station, Sheriff Station, Fire Station & DOT Station, and Civilian Spawn.
  11. Prohibited Roleplays: Suicide & Drug Roleplays are banned (Bomb RP is Allowed as long as you don't promote or simulate disasters/events which happened in real life). Your roleplay must comply with Roblox's Community Standards.
  12. Interfering with scenes: If you were not originally involved with someone else's scene, do not attach yourself to it.
  13. Group Limits: Do not go around the map in large groups (maximum of 6). You must also have at least 2+ people to rob the jewelry store and 3+ people to rob the bank.
  14. Banned Weapons: The weapons listed below cannot be used: Remington 870, Skorpian, Remington MSR (sniper), M249, Shotgun  (can't be used by any team).
  15. 15 Use of Liveries/Uniforms: It is strictly prohibited to utilize the default ERLC liveries/uniforms. It is mandatory to exclusively employ the liveries/uniforms that have been provided by our server.
  16. Hostage/Kidnap & Jewelry/Bank Robbery Roleplays: Prior to engaging in any roleplays, it is imperative that you seek permission from a staff member. Furthermore, it is strictly forbidden to abduct or forcefully detain any personnel affiliated with law enforcement (LEO), the Fire Department (FD), or the Department of Transportation (DOT).
  17. Metagaming: It is of utmost importance to adhere to a strict separation between in-character and out-of-character knowledge during roleplaying. Engaging in the utilization of information acquired outside the context of the roleplay is strictly prohibited.
  18.  Realistic Avatars: It is imperative that your avatar remains devoid of any head/body morphs or additional accessories, including but not limited to swords, wings, or any other similar elements.
  19. No Intention to Roleplay (NITRP): having no intentions on roleplaying Examples: Mass RDM/VDM Creating Troll Characters to target/harass other players Talking while dead or failing to RP a death.
  20. Loopholes: Please use common sense and do not try to find loopholes or mistakes within these rules.
